The Team

 

After we won the NASA Space Apps Challenge 2013 Global People’s Choice Award with our project Chicksbook, we wanted to work together on an existing challenge about a problem on Earth.

That’s why we – Pavel Kolev, Svetlio Toskov, Vladimir Zagorski, Stoyan Ivanov and Valentin Yanakiev took the Leaf Me Alone challenge and tried to solve it.
