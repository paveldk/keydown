The Solution

LeafZone Mobile Application

We created a mobile app for iOS, Android and Windows Phone 8 called Leaf Zone. We chose this name because we didnâ€™t want to focus on the ozone effect itself but rather engage people in discovering the plant life around them.

Take a Photo

With LeafZone, you can take a photo of a leaf and get instant analysis of the ozone damage on the leaf. You also get information about diseases affecting the plant. If the app cannot recognize a disease, you can easily report it. This can prevent the distribution of new diseases on Earth and, hopefully, one day on other planets we populate.

What plant you are looking at?

The app also shows the species to which the leaf belongs. You no longer need to wonder what plant you are looking at.

Discovered Leafs

You can review your discovered leafs and leafs discovered by other users.

Map of all recordings around the world

You can examine a map of all recordings around the world. The map shows the ozone pollution percentage in different regions.

Plants and Diseases

You can access a list of well-known plants and diseases, so you can quickly learn where to find a plant or disease, what are its characteristics, etc.

Personal Achievements

Discovering plants gives you achievement points. The more plants you discover, the higher you place on the leaderboard.
